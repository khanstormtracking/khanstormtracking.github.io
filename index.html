ABOUT US

Khan Storm Tracking is a YouTube channel made for Tropical Cyclone Animations and Archived Satellite Imagery. The project was originally made for Satellite Image collection but has now turned into mix of both animations and imagery. The project was founded on July 4, 2020 and has grew from there. The channel was abandoned until 2 months later in September where the founder; Khan made a test animation to test his abilities of video editing. It was rebranded from Storm Imagery to Khan Storm Tracking. There are now multiple team members and contributors in the project.

We hope that you find our videos helpful, educational and entertaining :)
